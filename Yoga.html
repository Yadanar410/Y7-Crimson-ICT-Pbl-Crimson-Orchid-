Yoga Tips for Beginners

  • Start with basic poses and gradually progress.
  • Focus on your breath; it’s the foundation of your practice.
  • Practice regularly, even if it’s just for a few minutes.
  • Listen to your body and don’t push yourself too hard.
  • Find a comfortable space to practice without distractions.
